Volunteer Opportunities

Very Important People
Christmas Celebration

Below is a description of our next event. Please join us and volunteer.

Hard to believe we are planning our 7th annual VIP Christmas Celebration!
I hope that you all had fun last year and would consider joining us again this year!  Or if you've never participated before I hope you can join us this year!
If you were not able to attend I hope that you would consider helping us this year. 
We have made a change to the timing of the event this year to 5:30pm-7:30pm.

The plans are shaping up...the invites have gone out... So now we need your help!
1.  Volunteers to make the night magical
2.  Donations of "gifts" for our table for the kids to pick after they visit with Santa.  Items would be like bouncy balls, fun bracelets or little cars. Items you might find at the Dollar Store, a party store or an online store with trinket items.  Any donation of any quantity would be great.  Our best guess is that we will have around 250 "kiddos" (remember many are adults) sitting on Santa's lap that night.

Please let me know if you plan to attend so that we can fill our volunteers spots by clicking for a "volunteer ticket"....(if you are unable to attend but would like to help us with the "gifts" for the events you may drop off any donations to the store).
 
 
A GINORMOUS thank you to Kids BMV....to the library for allowing us to invade for the event....to Chris Bucher for taking professional family photos this year....Behavior Analysis Center for Autism for cookies and milk, to Michael with Rosie's (the new restaurant coming to Main Street) for the "sweet" deal on the cookies and to everyone else for their contributions to make this night so meaningful for everyone!!!!!

 VIP Christmas Celebration for Special Needs Friends
“Very Important People” Christmas Celebration
This holiday celebration is for people with special needs and their families and caregivers.
 Friday December 5, 2014 5:30-7:30pm (Volunteers should arrive at 4:30PM)

The Hussey-Mayfield Memorial Public Library 250 N 5th Street, Zionsville 5:30-7:30pm Photos with Santa, games, story telling, sleigh rides, cookies, and more. Mark your calendars to join Santa, Mrs. Claus, and the elves for a special evening of fun and pictures at The Hussey-Mayfield Memorial Public Library on Friday December 5, 2014. The night will include free photos with Santa, story telling, games, and more. You never know where you might find Santa and his elves! Please RSVP today or call with any questions or ideas....looking forward to seeing you soon!
 Have a safe and happy holiday season!
